Magic The Gathering

Rules of Magic: The Gathering
  • Each player starts with 20 life.
  • Players take turns, starting with the first player.
  • Players draw one card at the beginning of their turn.
  • Players can play lands and cast spells during their turn.
  • Spells require mana to cast, which comes from lands.
  • Each player can attack with creatures to reduce the opponent's life.
  • The game is won by reducing the opponent's life to 0.
  • There are many more rules and card interactions to explore!
